## Design Context

### Users
Pet owners in Mexico who are members of "Club Pata Amiga". They use this widget to manage their pet memberships, view details about their benefits, and handle administrative tasks (like appeals or wait periods).

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Friendly, approachable, yet premium and trustworthy.
- **Tone**: "The Clubhouse Sanctuary". Balancing high-energy accents (Action Orange) with a calming, trustworthy base (Club Turquoise).
- **3-word personality**: Approachable, Premium, Vibrant.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Modern, glassmorphic, and organic.
- **Theme**: Light mode (Glass White backgrounds), with turquoise and orange accents.
- **Key Elements**: Extreme rounded corners (50px), glassmorphism (backdrop blur), soft shadows.

### Design Principles
1. **Round Confidence**: No sharp corners. Everything interactive or container-like should be rounded (15px-50px).
2. **Glassmorphic Depth**: Use semi-transparent backgrounds and blurs to create light, layered surfaces.
3. **Vibrant Precision**: Use Action Orange (#FE8F15) sparingly (≤10%) for primary calls to action.
4. **Editorial Typography**: Punchy Fraiche for headings/numbers, clean Outfit for body text.
