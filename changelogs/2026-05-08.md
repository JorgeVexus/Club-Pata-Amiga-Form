# Changelog - 2026-05-08

## [Solidarity Fund Dashboard] Stabilization & Eligibility Fixes

### Added
- `calculateCarencia` helper to `SolidarityDashboard` for accurate waiting period tracking.
- `getPetStatusContext` helper to handle administrative approval states and missing information.
- Dynamic "In carencia" and "Approved" badges in the pet list.

### Fixed
- **Photo Rendering**: Corrected photo fetching logic to prioritize `primary_photo_url` and provide a reliable fallback.
- **Business Rules**: Enforced strict validation: pets must be `approved` and past their `carencia` to be eligible for support.
- **UI Bug**: Hidden the "Add Pet" trigger when the maximum of 3 pets is reached.
- **Stats Consistency**: Updated frontend and backend to calculate "active" pets strictly based on both administrative approval AND completed carencia.
- **History UI**: Improved styling of the history search and filter dropdown, ensuring black text visibility and adding a custom arrow icon for a more premium look.

### Technical Details
- Modified `public/widgets/solidarity-dashboard.js`.
- Verified production build with `npm run build`.
