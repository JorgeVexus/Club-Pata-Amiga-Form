# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Pet owners in Mexico who want to secure a membership for their pets. They are often mobile-first, interacting with the system through a widget embedded in a Webflow site. They need a simple, reassuring flow to upload sensitive documents and track their membership status.

## Product Purpose
A comprehensive pet membership registration and management system. It facilitates onboarding (forms, document uploads, Stripe payments) and ongoing management (pet profiles, appeals, communication via "Pata Chat"). The goal is to provide a premium "club" experience for pet owners while maintaining an efficient, centralized dashboard for admins.

## Brand Personality
Friendly, Trusted, Premium.
The brand should evoke the warmth of a pet community with the technical reliability of a modern fintech service.

## Anti-references
- Standard Bootstrap or Material Design dashboards (too generic).
- Cluttered, bureaucratic government forms.
- Cold, text-heavy insurance interfaces.

## Design Principles
1. **Clubhouse Confidence**: Every interaction should feel like joining an exclusive club. Use friendly typography, soft corners, and a welcoming color palette.
2. **Transparent Lifecycle**: The "Waiting Period" and "Approval Status" are critical stress points. Use clear visual indicators and progress trackers to keep users informed.
3. **Unified Resolution**: Centralize all back-and-forth communication into the "Pata Chat". Avoid fragmented notifications; the chat is the single source of truth for resolving issues.
4. **Mobile-First Precision**: Since most users interact via an embedded widget, every UI element must be optimized for touch and narrow viewports without losing functional density.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for contrast and keyboard navigation.
- High-contrast states for pet approval statuses (Success green, Error red, Action yellow).
- Mobile-friendly tap targets (minimum 44x44px).
